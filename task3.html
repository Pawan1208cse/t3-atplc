Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. AI involves various subfields such as machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and robotics.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a subset of AI that focuses on enabling computers to learn and make decisions without being explicitly programmed. It involves algorithms and statistical models that allow machines to analyze and interpret large amounts of data, identify patterns, and make predictions or decisions based on that data.

Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing (NLP) deals with the interaction between computers and human language. It enables machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language, enabling tasks such as language translation, sentiment analysis, and voice recognition.

Computer Vision

Computer Vision involves teaching computers to understand and interpret visual information from images or videos. It enables machines to recognize objects, detect and analyze patterns, and make sense of visual data.

Robotics

Robotics combines AI and mechanical engineering to create intelligent machines or robots that can perform tasks autonomously or with human interaction. AI-powered robots are used in various fields, including manufacturing, healthcare, and exploration.
